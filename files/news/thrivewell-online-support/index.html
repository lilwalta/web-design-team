Introducing ThriveWell’s New Online Support Community

Published on Jan 28, 2025 • Platform Update

ThriveWell is excited to announce the launch of our new online support community—built to create a safe, welcoming space for people seeking connection, understanding, and guidance.

A Safe Space for Everyone

The platform includes moderated discussion boards, topic-based groups, and one-on-one peer matching. Every space is carefully monitored to maintain a safe, supportive environment.

Tools Designed for Real Support

Members gain access to journaling features, resource libraries, and the ability to connect with local groups and events. The goal is to create a comprehensive wellness hub that integrates with our larger ThriveWell ecosystem.
