Bipolar Disorder

Mood shifts between depression and mania that require long-term care.

Home › Disorders › Bipolar

Overview

Bipolar disorder causes intense emotional states known as manic, hypomanic, and depressive episodes.

Types

  • Bipolar I
  • Bipolar II
  • Cyclothymic disorder

Management

  • Mood-stabilizing medication
  • Psychotherapy
  • Sleep and stress regulation

Support

NAMI offers bipolar support groups nationwide.
