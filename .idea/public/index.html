About ThriveWell

At ThriveWell, we believe that mental health is just as vital as physical health. Our mission is to help individuals understand, manage, and embrace their mental well-being through education, resources, and community support.

We aim to de-stigmatize mental health challenges and empower everyone to seek the help they deserve — because no one should face their struggles alone.

You’re Not Alone

Reach out to trusted organizations and helplines that are ready to listen and help.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988 to connect with trained counselors anytime.

NAMI Helpline

Call 1-800-950-NAMI (6264) or text “HELPLINE” to 62640 for support.

SAMHSA Treatment Locator

Find local mental health and substance use treatment near you at findtreatment.gov.
