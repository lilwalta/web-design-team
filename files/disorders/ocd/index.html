What is OCD?

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder involves recurring, unwanted thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions) performed to reduce anxiety.

Symptoms

  • Intrusive thoughts or fears
  • Repetitive behaviors or rituals
  • Temporary relief followed by recurring anxiety

Causes

OCD is influenced by genetics, brain function, and environmental factors.

Treatment Options

  • Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP)
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • Medication such as SSRIs

Getting Help

Early treatment improves outcomes. OCD is manageable with professional support.
