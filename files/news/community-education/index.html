Community Mental Health Education Night

Home › News › Community Mental Health Education Night

Published on Mar 19, 2025 • Awareness & Education

Mental health experts host a virtual and in-person event focused on recognizing anxiety, depression, and ways to support loved ones struggling with emotional well-being.

How to Participate

On Saturday, March 22, you can join the event live either online via the ThriveWell Youtube account, or in-person at the City College of New York library building at 160 Convent Ave., New York, NY from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM (Dinner provided).

Who's Speaking

Two of the college's top psychology grad students have kindly offered to host the event and speak on a variety of topics focused on family and friend support systems.
