Highlights from the Youth Mental Health Summit

Published on Feb 2, 2025 • Awareness & Education

This year’s Youth Mental Health Summit brought together educators, counselors, and young leaders to discuss the growing mental health challenges faced by teens and young adults. Sessions focused on emotional resilience, social media pressure, and the increasing demand for accessible support.

Empowering the Next Generation

Workshops helped young attendees learn coping strategies, communication skills, and methods for recognizing when peers may need help. Mental health professionals emphasized the importance of early intervention.

Bridging the Gap

A recurring theme throughout the summit was the need for stronger school-based mental health programs. Attendees left with resources and action plans they could take back to their local communities.
